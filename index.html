Our Story

We Make Wedding Planning Easier

WeddingWala was founded by a team of Karachi-based tech entrepreneurs who experienced firsthand how stressful and time-consuming venue hunting could be. We built the platform we wished existed.

Today we serve 50,000+ couples annually across Pakistan with verified listings, real-time availability, and transparent pricing — no hidden fees, no surprises.

Our team of 40+ venue specialists personally visits every hall before it's listed, so you can browse with complete confidence.
